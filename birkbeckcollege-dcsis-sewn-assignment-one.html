SEWN Assignment One

This website forms part of the coursework for the Birkbeck College DCSIS SEWN assignment one. The aim is to design, create, and publish a website that is likely to be ranked highly in search engine results pages for specific queries. It should also contain some dynamic content. I have chosen to aggregate two RSS feeds for the dynamic content to produce the Tech News.

SEWN

SEWN stands for Search Engines and Web Navigation. It is a module taught by Mark Levene who has a book published on the subject called "An Introduction to Search Engines and Web Navigation".

As you will be aware, search engines have become an indispensable tool for web users.

Birkbeck College

Birkbeck College is part of The University of London. It is London's only specialist provider of evening higher education, which is useful if you want to continue to work full time as well as study.

DCSIS

DCSIS stands for the Department of Computer Science and Information Systems at Birkbeck College. They offer a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate and research degrees, as well as skills based courses. Check out the DCSIS web site.

MSc Computer Science

The MSc Computer Science is an intensive course for graduates of subjects other than computing wishing to move into a career in technology, or people already working in IT and seeking to broaden their knowledge and update their skills. Check out the MSc Computer Science web page.
